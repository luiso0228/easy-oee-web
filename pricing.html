FAQ

COMMON QUESTIONS.

Is there a free trial?+
Yes. Every plan starts with a 14-day free trial — no credit card required. You get full access to the plan you choose, with real data from your floor. Cancel any time before the trial ends and you won't be charged.
Can I change plans later?+
Yes, at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. No penalties, no fine print.
Is there a setup fee?+
No. Never. Not for any plan, at any time. The price you see is the price you pay.
Do you offer annual billing discounts?+
Yes — 20% off when you pay annually. That's two months free compared to monthly billing. Toggle the switch above to see annual prices.
Do you offer discounts for multiple plants?+
Yes. If you're managing two or more facilities, contact us for multi-plant pricing. Enterprise plans can cover unlimited locations under a single contract.
What happens when I cancel?+
You keep access until the end of your billing period. After that, your account is paused and your data is preserved for 30 days so you can come back without losing anything. Professional and Enterprise users can export data at any time.
Do I need to sign a long-term contract?+
No. Monthly plans are month-to-month. Annual plans are billed once per year. Cancel any time — no commitments, no penalties.
