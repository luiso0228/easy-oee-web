Where do OEE losses come from?

THREE TYPES OF LOSSES. ALL MEASURABLE.

Most plant managers are surprised to find out which category is costing them the most. Easy OEE makes all three visible in real time.

01
Availability Losses
Unplanned downtime

The machine stops when it shouldn't. Mechanical failures, waiting for materials, waiting for operators. Every minute of unplanned stop is 100% lost production — and every one needs a reason logged.

02
Performance Losses
Running below ideal speed

The machine is running, but slower than it should be. Small stops, micro-stoppages, reduced speed for quality reasons. These losses are invisible without tracking — which is why most plants underestimate them.

03
Quality Losses
Making parts that fail

Parts that have to be reworked or scrapped. The machine ran, the time was used, but the output has no value. Quality losses are deceptively expensive because the cost of materials and labour is already spent.
